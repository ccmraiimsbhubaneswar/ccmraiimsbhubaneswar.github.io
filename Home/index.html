Introduction

❮ ❯


CENTER OF EXCELLENCE FOR CLINICAL MICROBIOME RESEARCH (CCMR) is dedicated to catalyzing human microbiome research and initiating and providing safe and standardized practice of fecal microbiota transplant (FMT) and. This institute is the first to host a center focussing on Clinical Microbiome research and bringing together and will be host to clinicians, scientists & public health practitioners.

The main objectives of this institute will be:

1. To accelerate translational human microbiome research: CCMR will provide opportunities to perform microbiome research and apply these findings to improve human health. CCMR is being developed as a state-of-the-art facility to unlock this potential. The facility will provide metagenomics, metabolomics, bioinformatics, storage, delivery, and all regulatory services. It will focus on developing personalized care based on the Microbiome of an individual. Microbiome studies across populations will provide a better understanding of its role in health and disease.

2. To provide safe and standardized fecal Microbiota transplantation: FMT is all about transplanting the gut microbiome from a healthy person into a patient’s gastrointestinal tract; such a transplant will reconstruct the gut microbial ecosystem and treat the underlying gastrointestinal tract disease. It is an emerging treatment strategy for multiple conditions that are associated with enteric dysbiosis. CCMR will strive to provide every patient requiring FMT to have safe, convenient, and affordable access. It will make the path to treatment straightforward for both patients and physicians by bringing a level of safety and standardization to the process. It will implement rigorous donor & recipient screening, provide ready-to-use fecal microbiota preps and post-transplant follow-up. This will give physicians more time to devote to patient care than the complexities of managing fecal donor banks.
